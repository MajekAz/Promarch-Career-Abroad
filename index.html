Pre-Application Checklist

1

International Passport

Ensure it has at least 6 months validity. Apply at Nigeria Immigration Service early.

2

Academic Transcripts

Get official transcripts from your university. If you have an HND, you might need a Top-up or Pre-masters.

3

English Proficiency

Most universities accept WAEC/NECO C6 in English (within last 5-10 years). Otherwise, IELTS is required.

Understanding Proof of Funds (POF)

To get your CAS, you must show you have the Tuition Balance + Living Expenses in a bank account for 28 consecutive days.

  • London Area: £1,334 per month (up to 9 months)
  • Outside London: £1,023 per month (up to 9 months)

Promarch can help calculate the exact amount you need in Naira based on current exchange rates.

Common Questions

Do you help with Proof of Funds (POF)?

Yes, we provide guidance on how to keep your funds for the required 28-day period and can calculate exactly how much you need in Naira based on the current exchange rate.

How long does the UK Student Visa take?

Standard service takes about 15 working days (3 weeks) after your biometric appointment. Priority services are available for faster decisions (5-7 days).

Can I bring my family?

As of January 2024, only students on research-based postgraduate courses (PhDs/Research Masters) can bring dependents. We can advise on alternative pathways.
